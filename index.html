Biography

Akintunde is a Cloud Engineering (CE) student in Altschool Africa with student ID number: ALT/SOE/023/3017. I joined Altschool Africa due to my passion and interest in Cloud Computing, the continuous innovations is one of the factors that thrills me, I must confess.
